Hello! Whuru?

When the web asks, "Who are you?", Whuru helps you answer.

About You

Whuru lets you build a custom page to help humans discover things about you. Coming soon

Webfinger

Whuru uses the WebFinger protocol to help machines discover things about you. Coming soon

Persona

Whuru enables you to log into sites using Mozilla Persona. Coming soon

Email

Persona and Webfinger IDs look like email addresses. So, Whuru will forward email sent to that address to an inbox of your choice. Maybe someday

Custom Domains

If you own your own domain name, you can control your identity and delegate services to Whuru. Maybe someday
